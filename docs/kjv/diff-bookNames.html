Names of many bible books from Gutenberg were shortened. For example, Genesis was used instead of "The First Book of Moses: Called Genesis". Names remembered from Gutenberg which may have been shortened are listed in table 2. Some confusion occurred when doing a text search on one book title "The First Book of the Kings" in the Gutenberg etext since multiple results were observed:
  • The First Book of Samuel Otherwise Called: The First Book of the Kings
  • The First Book of the Kings Commonly Called: The Third Book of the Kings.
