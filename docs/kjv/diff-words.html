Observations of different words are summarized below. Most things are minor, but even minor differences can make a checksum on the files ineffective. It was uncertain if there were personal typos or if a few individual anomalies were changed after someone was reporting them. This was because the Gutenberg etext was saved from years back and a couple of word differences were not found in the etext on the net. A hyphen (-) means no differences observed.
